I'm Jakob Giggenbach, a dedicated photography student with a focus on Foto-Design at the University of Applied Sciences in Munich. My creative heart beats for the intersection of Fashion, Product Photography, and Film. I seek to weave narratives into every shot, blending the elegance of fashion with the precision of product aesthetics. My films extend these stories into dynamic sequences, filled with emotion and expression. The addition of CGI allows me to construct imaginative realities that enhance and elevate my visual storytelling. For opportunities to collaborate or to see more of my work, please reach out.
